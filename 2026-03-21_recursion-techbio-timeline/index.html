Recursion Pharmaceuticals: the TechBio paradigm — from $41 peak to $3 reckoning
RXRX stock price (Apr 2021–Mar 2026) with key capital events and pipeline milestones
Recursion Pharmaceuticals IPO'd in April 2021 at $18, reached an all-time high of $41.33 within three months, and has since fallen 92% to below $4 — a trajectory that mirrors the entire arc of the TechBio thesis. The company built one of the world's largest biological datasets, struck partnerships worth up to $12B with Roche/Genentech and Sanofi, and absorbed UK rival Exscientia for $688M. Yet clinical data has been mixed and the cost of running an AI-native drug factory at scale has relentlessly consumed capital. NVIDIA — which made a $50M strategic investment in 2023 — quietly sold its entire 7.71 million-share position by December 2025. Cathie Wood's ARK now holds 21.8 million shares, nearly three times NVIDIA's former stake. The divergence between NVIDIA's exit and ARK's accumulation marks two opposite readings of the same evidence — one closing its position near the floor, the other treating the same price as an entry point.
