One million Lebanese displaced in sixteen days
Cumulative people internally displaced in Lebanon since the start of the Iran war, thousands
When US and Israeli forces launched Operation Epic Fury on February 28, 2026, Lebanon — still reeling from economic collapse, the 2020 Beirut port explosion, and years of political paralysis — became an immediate casualty. Within 16 days, more than one million people had fled their homes: roughly one in five of Lebanon's 5.5 million population. The pace was extraordinary: 700,000 in the first week alone. With 4.1 million Lebanese already in humanitarian need before the war began, the new displacement has overwhelmed collective shelters and aid systems that were already stretched to breaking point.
Sources: UNHCR, IOM Displacement Tracking Matrix, UNICEF, UN OCHA — Lebanon Situation Reports, Feb–Mar 2026  |  @enriqui
